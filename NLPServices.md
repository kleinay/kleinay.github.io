## Core Offerings

### 1. Custom LLM Engineering & Domain Adaptation
Off-the-shelf models struggle with highly domain-specific jargon & nuances, specialized academic coding schemes, and strict data privacy requirements. I build custom, locally-hosted LLM pipelines tailored to your specific needs --- delivering high-precision results with 100% data privacy.
* **Efficient Fine-Tuning (PEFT/LoRA):** Training compact, open-weight models (like Llama or Mistral) on your private datasets. 
* **Cost & Latency Reduction:** Slashing cloud API costs by deploying smaller, highly capable models that rival massive commercial counterparts in your specific domain.
* **Data Privacy & IP Ownership:** Delivering custom model weights that you own completely, ensuring sensitive data (medical, corporate, or academic) never leaves your servers.

### 2. Deep-Text Analytics
Most data science tools are built for numbers; I build tools for human language. I specialize in parsing highly nuanced text into structured statistical tables ready for analysis.
* **Feature Extraction:** Identifying custom, structured properties (e.g., clinical/psychological markers, behavioral signals, user intents, or organizational sentiment) from any textual domain --- open-ended surveys, therapy transcripts, customer support logs, and social media.
* **Clustering & Pattern Recognition:** Applying advanced semantic representations and unsupervised learning to map massive text corpora into distinct themes or "personas" without relying on manual human coding.

### 3. Methodological Validation & Automated Annotation
For academic labs and data-driven startups, the validity of the data is everything. I implement rigorous validation pipelines that peer-reviewers and stakeholders trust.
* **LLM-as-a-Judge Workflows:** Designing robust evaluation systems to grade model outputs or score human text based on complex rubrics.
* **Automated Prompt Optimization:** Systematically tuning prompts to maximize Inter-Annotator Agreement (IAA) against your human-labeled Gold Standard datasets.
